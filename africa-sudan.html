The Rapid Support Forces (RSF) consolidated complete control over West Kordofan state in Sudan on December 8, 2025, capturing Heglig oil facility processing 130,000 barrels daily.

The RSF's control represents a critical shift in the civil conflict's territorial balance.

The RSF has conducted systematic ethnically motivated attacks resulting in over 200 deaths in North Darfur.
