About Our Source Spectrum

The left-center-right placement is approximate and based on widely recognized media bias assessments. We reference multiple sources across the spectrum to provide readers with diverse perspectives on each event. The marker position on each article represents the aggregate balance of sources consulted, not a judgment of the article's own bias.
