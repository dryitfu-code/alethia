How Alethia News Works

Alethia News is an independent news platform designed to present global events with clarity, structure, and transparency.

Our Approach

Articles are categorized by region and topic, allowing readers to quickly access relevant information without algorithmic filtering or personalization bias. We present news from multiple geographical perspectives to provide context often missing from single-region coverage.

Source Transparency

For major stories, we reference multiple news outlets across the political spectrum. Each article links to our Sources page, where readers can see which outlets we consulted and where they fall on a left-center-right editorial spectrum. This isn't a judgment of quality or accuracy—it's transparency about perspective diversity.

What We Prioritize

  • Accuracy and verification over speed
  • Context and background over breaking headlines
  • Source diversity over single perspectives
  • Clarity and readability over jargon
  • Transparency about our methods

What We Don't Do

  • We don't use algorithmic personalization
  • We don't hide our source selection
  • We don't claim perfect objectivity
  • We don't prioritize engagement metrics over substance
  • We don't accept advertising or sponsored content

Alethia (ἀλήθεια) is the Greek word for "truth" or "disclosure." We chose this name to reflect our commitment to transparency in how news is gathered and presented.
